UI/UX Design

UI/UX design is the process of creating user interfaces and user experiences for digital products, such as websites, mobile apps, and software applications. UI (user interface) design focuses on the visual design of the product, while UX (user experience) design focuses on how users interact with the product and how it meets their needs.
